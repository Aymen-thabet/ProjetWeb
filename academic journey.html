Military Education

In 2016, I embarked on my educational journey at the Military Academy in Fondok Jedid after completing my math baccalaureate.

Over the subsequent years, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Physics and Chemistry (2016-2019) and pursued Military Science, earning a Master's degree with six months of intensive infantry training (2019-2021).

Graduating in 2021 with the rank of Lieutenant, I served as a Platoon Leader.

I furthered my expertise with an Excellence Certification in Military Intelligence and Security, involving an additional six months of rigorous study (2021-2022).
