Military Education

From 2016 to 2019, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Physics and Chemistry.

Between 2019 and 2021, I delved into the realm of Military Science, earning a Master's degree that included six months of intensive infantry combat training.

In 2021, I proudly graduated from the military academy, attaining the rank of Lieutenant and taking on the role of a Platoon Leader.

During 2021-2022, I furthered my expertise with an Excellence Certification in Military Intelligence and Security, involving six months of rigorous studies.
